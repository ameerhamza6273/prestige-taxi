About Our Company

Providing reliable taxi services in Beaconsfield for over 45 years.

A Legacy of Trust & Safety

Established in 1979, Fleet Taxis has grown from a small local operator to one of the most trusted transportation providers in Buckinghamshire. For over four decades, we have remained committed to our core values: punctuality, safety, and professional service.

We understand that a taxi service is more than just a ride—it's about getting you to your flight on time, ensuring you get home safely late at night, and providing a dependable link for our community.
